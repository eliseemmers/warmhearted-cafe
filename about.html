Warmhearted Cafe is a new friendly coffee shop located in Milwaukee, WI. Escape and relax to the heart of the city at Warmhearted Cafe. Enjoy our cozy casual atmosphere. Freshly baked goods and tasty cafe classics made daily. We use the finest coffee beans from around the world Kona coffee beans (Hawaii), Blue Mountain Coffee (Jamaica) Peaberry Coffee beans (Tanzania). Kona Coffee beans are considered some of best in the world, with a mellow flavor and bright acidity. Blue Mountain Coffee beans create coffee that is smooth with a sweet flavor. The taste is for the refined palate, rich and almost creamy. Peaberry coffee beans are a medium bodied roast with high acidity, and are considered exotic. The taste is fruity and floral, with a sweet finish.
